About RAAG the Band

RAAG the Band, based in Bangalore, has been captivating audiences worldwide since 2005 with their charismatic vibes, breathtaking performances, and hypnotizing voices. Comprised of musicians skilled in Indian classical and Western music, they have already delivered over 7000 shows. Our dedicated team of imaginative professionals crafts unparalleled entertainment exclusively for you, blending Indian and Western music seamlessly. We prioritize quality and your preferences, boasting a competitive edge and years of industry experience that make us an ideal partner.

By entrusting RAAG the Band, we consistently exceed expectations, regardless of the circumstances. We prioritize relationships with corporates, business firms, organizations, private companies, and individuals. We are fortunate to feature two exceptionally talented multilingual singers who have graced stages globally for over a decade. Their diverse repertoire effortlessly enchants audiences with renditions in Hindi, English, Kannada, Tamil, Malayalam, and Telugu, bringing immense joy and representing musical excellence.
